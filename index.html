OVERVIEW

THE MOTHER CONTINENT IS WHERE WE ALL CAME FROM. THOSE WHO VISIT ARE RETURNING, AND FOR THOSE WHO LIVE HERE, THERE'S SO MUCH MORE TO SEE.

Two perfect meetings of wildlife and a life of luxury. One on the edge of a cliff, with views that take your breath away. Another on the sands of the Zambezi, where luxury tents and river adventures await. We invite you to explore two truly unique African experiences.

GORGES LODGE

Gorges Lodge is built on the edge of Batoka Gorge, with spectacular views of the Zambezi River below. There are 10 thatched chalets set amongst the lush indigenous gardens of the Lodge, offering options of double-storey suites with wide sheltered balconies and single-storey suites with private verandas. These luxury rooms boast en-suite bathrooms and you can cool off any time of day in the lodge’s swimming pool.
